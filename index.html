About SunRise-Donation

SunRise-Donation is a transparent, community-driven platform that empowers NGOs to share their causes, receive donations, and track every rupee spent. Our mission is simple — connect donors with real stories and create measurable impact in communities that need it the most.

Whether it's funding education, providing healthcare, or feeding the hungry, we ensure your contributions directly reach those in need — and you can see the difference you make.
